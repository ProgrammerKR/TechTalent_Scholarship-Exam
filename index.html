About the Scholarship

The Tech Talent Scholarship Exam offers young students aged 8 to 25 a chance to shine in fields such as coding, artificial intelligence, and software development. Organized by Prog. Kanishk Raj, this program nurtures young talent, providing financial and technical support to help students shape the future of technology.

Note: This exam is only for Indian students. Participants must be Indian citizens to apply.

Eligibility & Details

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 8-25 years old
  • Academic Background: Enrolled in school, college, or university
  • Technology Passion: Interest in programming, AI, robotics, etc.
  • Nationality: Indian citizens only
  • Proof of Age: Birth certificate, school ID
  • Academic Report: Latest report card
  • Personal Essay: 500 words on your tech aspirations

How to Apply

Follow the steps below to apply for the Tech Talent Scholarship Exam and secure your spot.

  • Step 1: Register online at Here
  • Step 2: Fill out the form with personal details.
  • Step 3: Submit the form.
  • Step 4: Receive an email with a QR Code, UPI ID, and mobile number for payment.
  • Step 5: Complete the payment process.
  • Step 6: Upon successful payment, your registration will be confirmed.
